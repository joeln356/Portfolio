01. About Me

I am a Computer Science student passionate about the intersection between software development and cybersecurity. My journey started by building web applications, but quickly evolved into understanding how to break and secure them.


I believe in the Secure-by-Design philosophy. It is not enough for code to simply work; it must withstand adversaries in an ever-evolving threat landscape. That’s why I combine my Full-Stack skills with penetration testing methodologies.


When I’m not coding or configuring Docker containers, you can find me hunting for vulnerabilities in Bug Bounty programs, participating in CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions, or exploring machines on HackTheBox and TryHackMe.
