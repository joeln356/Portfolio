01. About Me

I am a Computer Science student passionate about the intersection between software development and cybersecurity. My journey started by building web applications, but quickly evolved into understanding how to break and protect them.


I believe in the Secure-by-Design philosophy. It's not enough for the code to work; it must withstand adversaries in an ever-evolving threat landscape. That's why I combine my Web Development skills with pentesting methodologies.


When I'm not coding or configuring Docker containers, you'll find me hunting vulnerabilities in Bug Bounty programs, participating in CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions, or grinding machines on HackTheBox and TryHackMe.
